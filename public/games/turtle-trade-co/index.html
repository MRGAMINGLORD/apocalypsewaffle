How to Play

  • Controls: Use WASD to Move. Use your Mouse to look around. SPACE to jump!
  • Interaction: Press B to Interact (Chop trees, collect pebbles/tips, pick up turtles, use checkout, interact with boats and stairs).
  • Chatting: Press B closely while looking at a customer to talk to them. Use number keys to reply!
  • Upgrades: Press E to open the Upgrade Menu to build your shop, buy stands, and expand.
  • Expeditions: Build a boat to manually sail around the sea! Explore the fog of war to find Wild Islands.
  • Map: While sailing, press E to open the Map.
  • Managers: Hire a manager to automatically take care of the island, but they take $1000 daily.
